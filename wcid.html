How to help

Everyday activities all add up to help improve the Earth's temperature and reduce Global Warming!
These are all simple things but all make a difference


1.

Can you and your pals organise to walk to school if nearby. You could organise a buddy system. Parents could take it in turns to join you.

2.

If you can’t walk then start a car pool, saving money and saving using more fossil fuels!

3.

On the weekend , make sure to get out and cycle your bicycle, great fun and great for reducing the GreenHouse Gases.

4.

Recycle bottles , jars, paper. Organise to split out your recycling at home, everyone can help.

5.

Then make sure to also have a food waste for the kitchen, this then can be used in your new compost in the garden!

6.

Remember when shopping to bring your bags to the shops.

7.

And when out shopping remember to shop local, by buying stuff produced close by, you reduce the global footprint as the food does not have to be transported.

8.

Make sure to turn off the Tv and other switches when you don’t need them instead of putting on standby. If we all do it , that means our global footprint would come right down.

9.

Water can be saved by having a shower instead of a bath.

10.

Think up fun ways to create energy , skate , rollerblade . Why not organise an event with your classmates or friends with some fun event like an organised cycle. You can upload your video here and a special badge will be awarded.
